The Story Behind ROX

How one bad experience sparked a mission to transform online trading across Africa

"I lost money to a scam, but I found a purpose: to make sure no one else has to experience that pain."

— Cohen Ngoepe, Founder of ROX Buy and Sell

The Story Behind ROX

It all started with a simple online purchase—a laptop. Like many people, our founder, Cohen Ngoepe, was excited to find a great deal. He sent the payment, trusting the seller to deliver. But the laptop never came. Instead, he was left with an empty wallet and a hard lesson: online marketplaces aren't always safe.

"That moment of frustration and betrayal" — the pain millions across Africa know too well

The moment online trading scams in Africa became personal

That moment of frustration and betrayal became the spark for ROX Buy and Sell. Cohen realized he wasn't alone—millions of people across Africa face the same risks every day. Scams, unreliable sellers, and platforms that don't protect users are far too common.

But instead of accepting this as "just the way it is," Cohen decided to do something about it. He gathered a team of passionate individuals who shared his vision: to create a marketplace where safety, trust, and community come first.

From Victims to Champions

Empowering traders across Africa to succeed

ROX Buy and Sell isn't just a response to one bad experience—it's a commitment to ensuring that no one else has to go through what Cohen did. It's about building a better future for online trading in Africa, one secure transaction at a time.

Why Build ROX Buy and Sell

Most online marketplaces are designed to maximize profits, not protect users. They're reactive, not proactive. They let anyone sign up without verification, allow scams to flourish, and leave users to fend for themselves when things go wrong.

ROX is different. We're built on the principle that trust isn't optional—it's essential. We're not here to simply connect buyers and sellers; we're here to create a safe, supportive ecosystem where everyone can trade with confidence.

From verified accounts to real-time fraud detection, from secure payments to dedicated customer support, every feature of ROX is designed with your safety and success in mind.
