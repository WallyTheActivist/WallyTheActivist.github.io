About the Founder

Cohen Ngoepe

Founder & CEO

What if there was a marketplace Africa could trust?

Cohen Ngoepe is the visionary behind ROX Buy and Sell. After experiencing firsthand the dangers of unregulated online marketplaces, Cohen set out to create a platform that puts safety, transparency, and community at the forefront.

Driven by a passion for technology and a deep commitment to empowering African entrepreneurs, Cohen has assembled a team of talented professionals dedicated to transforming the way people buy and sell online.

Cohen's Approach

  • Experienced fraud firsthand, fueling a mission to protect others
  • Believes in building products that solve real problems for real people
  • Committed to transparency, integrity, and innovation
  • Passionate about supporting small businesses and local entrepreneurs
  • Guided by faith and a desire to create positive impact across Africa
  • Focused on building a platform where everyone can trade with confidence
